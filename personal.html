I was born on October 15, 2002 in Miami Florida. Growing up, I quickly became invested in art. The endless possibilities of what I could create excited me. As I grew older, I became aware of more powerful tools that allowed for more types of expression. Technology opened my eyes to the fact that art has a plethora of digital mediums to explore. Since then my interests have fallen towards computer animation, digital painting, game design, 3D modeling, and more. I hope to eventually become proficient in as many of them as I can. Technology is such a massive part of my life, and I want to become extremely effecient in using it to its fullest potential.
