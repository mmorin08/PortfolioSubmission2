I was born on October 15th 2002 in Miami Florida. From a very young age, I became invested in drawing. Being able to create anything I could imagine on a sheet of paper excited me. As I grew older, I became aware of more powerful tools that allowed for more types of expression. Technology opened my eyes to the fact that art has a large amount of digital mediums to explore, and I wanted to experience them all. My interests lie in computer animation, game design, 3D modeling, and so much more. Additionally, Technology is such a massive part of my life, and I want to become extremely effecient in using it to its fullest potential. Besides art, some of my hobbies include skateboarding, tennis, and gaming.
