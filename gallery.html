Cave,(2021)

Digital painting done on Procreate for iPad. This piece was an exercise in rendering darker lit areas, and contrast between cool and warm colors.

Field,(2021)

Digital painting done on Procreate for iPad. Depicts a lone cherry blossom in a field. I aimed to make the field have a sense of serenity, but at the same time chaos.

Hands,(2020)

Digital painting done on Procreate for iPad. This piece was a departure from my more painted style. I utilized varied digital pencil strokes to create an organic but surreal landscape. Realism was less of a focus for me in this piece.

Logs,(2020)

Digital painting done on Procreate for iPad. This piece was an exercise in perspective, and how values were altered depending on their distance to the viewer. The piece depicts a dreary forest with mysterious massive logs towering over the landscape.

Spikes,(2020)

Digital painting done on Procreate for iPad. In this piece I sought to create a landscape that felt surrealistic, yet grounded in reality. The spiky formations on the ground are odd and all point in the same direction, adding to the sense of mystery.
